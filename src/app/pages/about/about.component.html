Our Story Started With a Passion for Cars

DriveX was founded in 2016 with a clear vision: to provide an exceptional car rental experience that combines luxury with affordable prices. We started with a small fleet of 10 cars, and today we own over 150 cars from the finest global brands.

We believe that every journey begins with the right choice, which is why we are committed to offering a diverse fleet that meets all needs and different budgets, while maintaining full compliance with quality and regular maintenance for every car.
